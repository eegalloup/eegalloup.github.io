About The Luthier Toolbox

Crafted by Luthiers for Luthiers

The Luthier Toolbox is a collection of tools designed to assist luthiers in their craft. Developed with the expertise of the Galloup School, this suite brings together years of experience to create a user-friendly platform where builders can engage in the science of lutherie.

Luthier Toolbox simplifies complex tasks, enabling luthiers to focus on what matters most: creating exceptional instruments. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting out, The Luthier Toolbox is here to support your journey.
